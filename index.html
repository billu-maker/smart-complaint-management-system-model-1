Core Platform Capabilities

AI-Assisted Grievance Redressal

Engineered with natural language processing and smart workflow routing to reduce resolution delay and eliminate departmental handoff blockages.

NLP Categorization

Advanced Natural Language Processing reads your complaint description to categorize the grievance automatically, skipping manual typing delays.

Priority Assessment

Hazard evaluation heuristics analyze risk-prone keywords (e.g., "exposed cables", "flooding") to elevate priority status instantly.

Operational Blueprint

How It Works

Standard operating procedures ensuring fast and honest local grievance resolution.

1

Citizen Submission

Citizen registers the grievance with ward number, location name, description, and an optional site image.

2

AI Assessment

SCMS server model extracts key tags, classifies primary departments, and alerts on public safety hazards.

3

Division Dispatch

Instantly routes and logs the ticket directly to the respective engineering division's officer dashboard.

4

Officer Action

The assigned engineer inspects the spot, logs detailed diagnostics, and schedules physical repair works.

5

Physical Resolution

Municipal field crews or authorized contractors execute repairs on the site, updating logs dynamically.

6

Public Verification

Repaired status is logged along with proof of completion. The record is permanently updated in our statistics board.

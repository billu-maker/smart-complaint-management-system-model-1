Core Platform Capabilities

AI-Powered Governance

Engineered with deep learning algorithms and smart workflow routing to reduce resolution times and eliminate departmental friction.

AI Complaint Categorization

Advanced Natural Language Processing (NLP) immediately reads your description to categorize the grievance without human entry.

Smart Priority Detection

Deep hazard severity grading system assesses public risks and elevates urgent pipelines (e.g. major flooding, open wires) automatically.

Operational Blueprint

How It Works

A completely integrated automated pipeline resolving complaints transparently.

1

Citizen Submission

Citizen registers complaint with location coordinates, ward information, and photo attachment on our portal.

2

AI Processing

SCMS deep engine analyzes complaint texts, assesses severity factors, and classifies the primary category.

3

Department Assignment

Instantly routes and registers the complaint record directly to the respective engineer's action board.

4

Officer Acceptance

Assigned administrative officer performs field validation, approves details, and schedules inspection.

5

Work In Progress

Authorized field workers and contractor teams execute physical restorations. Real-time logging of timeline events.

6

Resolution Closed

On-site resolution images are uploaded. Citizen validates outcomes, triggers automated closure logs.
